Whitesnake is jovial and upbeat. Despite his eternally-dripping pelt, he's a rather outgoing and friendly tom. He's playful... Especially with what he considers prey. He genuinely believes there is nothing wrong with bloodshed, and is prone to taking things much too far when sparring or training.

Backstory

An ex-Shadowclan warrior who was exiled for going too hard during sparring with apprentices, often leaving them bloody and preferring to let them "figure things out on their own" when predators would appear during training sessions. Rather than face punishment, fled the clan and then drowned in the deep marshes after a misstep trying to evade his former clanmates. Sent to the Dark Forest, thinks very lightly of lives and enjoys bloodshed.
