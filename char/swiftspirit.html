Eleos' body is soft white fur that fades to lilac lynx points on her face, legs and tail, with white paws except for her left rear paw. Her eyes are a light, sky blue in color, and she is just slightly above average height. She has the long limbs of a runner and the lean body to match. Her nose and pawpads are a light, dusty pink.

There is a single clawmark across her left shoulder, and a v-shaped nick on her left ear. A long set of clawmarks stretches across her underside. Her fur is short but thick.
