Birdsong is tall for the average cat, around 15in at the shoulder. She's slender with long legs and the typical well-muscled haunches of a Skyclan cat. Her pelt is yet unmarked by scars, and is that of a medium-gray classic tabby with slightly darker stripes. There is slightly paler fur along her muzzle, chest, belly and paws, but no true whitespotting to be found. Her fur is long and silky, but often slightly unkempt. Her eyes are a soft, pale green.
