Backstory

Eosphoros, or Eos as she's commonly known by, was born to rogue parents in a land far away with two siblings, Casper and Dragon. While the kits were young, the Asylum took over their family's lands during a harsh leafbare. They kept sickness at bay, kept cats fed and kept the predators away. Eos quickly joined the scout faction with her brother Casper, while Dragon joined the Nightstrikers, and the trio have served the Asylum faithfully ever since.
